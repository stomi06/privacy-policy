Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the app "DevoWave Conversion Insights" ("the App") collects, uses, and stores data when installed by a Shopify merchant.

Data We Collect

The App collects only minimal data required to provide analytics functionality:

  • Product data: product_id
  • Aggregated statistics: views, add to cart events, purchases
  • Order data (via Shopify webhooks):
    • product_id
    • quantity

The App does NOT collect or store:

  • Customer names
  • Email addresses
  • Shipping or billing addresses
  • Payment information
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

How We Use Data

The collected data is used solely to:

  • Analyze product performance
  • Calculate conversion rates
  • Identify drop-off points in the purchase process

Data Storage

Data is stored securely on our servers. Only aggregated statistics are stored, and no individual user tracking is performed.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service. Merchants may request deletion of their data at any time.

Third-Party Services

The App may use secure third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting and database services) to operate. No personal data is shared with third parties.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, including secure HTTPS communication and restricted access to stored data.

Merchant Rights

Merchants may:

  • Request access to their data
  • Request deletion of their data
